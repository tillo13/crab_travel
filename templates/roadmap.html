Roadmap

Where we are, where we're going, and what matters most. Leave feedback on any section — it helps us prioritize.

Stage 1 — Live Now

Trip Creation & Invites shipped

Create a trip, share a link, anyone can join without signing up.

Organizer creates trip with destinations, dates, headcount
Unique invite link (crab.travel/to/XXXX) — no app download
Members join and fill preferences (budget, interests, dietary, mobility)
Anonymous or Google-authenticated participation

Voting & Availability shipped

Group decision-making that actually works.

Rank-order destination voting (#1, #2, #3)
Date availability calendar with color-coded preferences (ideal / if needed / can't go)
Inline date range picker
Member-suggested destinations with coordinator approval
Trip stages (Voting / Planning / Booked)

AI Destination Research shipped

Claude researches each destination and builds a Pinterest-style board.

AI-generated destination cards (stays, activities, food, events)
Compatibility scoring based on group preferences
Group vibes override (e.g. "tailgating" filters all recommendations)
"Suggest for us" — AI proposes 5 destinations from group profile

Travel Search & Deals shipped

Real flight, hotel, and activity prices from multiple sources.

4 search adapters running in parallel (Duffel, LiteAPI, Viator, Travelpayouts)
Streaming results via SSE — prices appear as they come in
Global deals dashboard refreshed daily
Deep links to booking partners (affiliate revenue)

Chat & Notifications shipped

Keep the group in the loop without a group text.

Trip chat with threaded replies
Email notifications (real-time, daily digest, weekly digest)
SMS notifications — pending Twilio A2P campaign approval
Notification channel preferences per user

Stage 2 — Up Next

Price Drop Alerts next

Monitor flights and hotels for locked-in destinations

"Best time to book" recommendation based on price trends

Per-Person Cost Breakdown next

No more spreadsheets tracking who owes what.

Running total per person (flight + hotel + activities)
Organizer marks shared vs. individual expenses
Venmo/Zelle deep links for easy settlement
Visual "who's paid / who owes" dashboard

Day-by-Day Itinerary next

Turn the pin board into an actual schedule.

Drag activities into day slots from the destination board
Shared timeline view — everyone sees the plan

Room Assignments & Flight Tracking next

The stuff group organizers currently track in a PDF.

SMS Voting & Replies next

Not everyone will log in. Meet them where they are.

Reply "1" for Nashville, "2" for Phoenix — vote via text
Inbound SMS commands (confirm attendance, vote, RSVP)
Chat messages forwarded to SMS, replies flow back into chat

Email Digest for Lurkers next

Weekly recap that keeps everyone engaged without nagging.

"3 new votes — Nashville is winning. Flights dropped to $189."
