Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 8, 2026

1. Who We Are

crab.travel ("we", "us", "our") is a group travel coordination platform. This policy applies to crab.travel and all related services.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly, including:

  • Account information (name, email, profile photo) via Google Sign-In
  • Profile data you enter (home airport, travel preferences, dietary needs, phone number)
  • Trip data (destinations, votes, availability, chat messages)
  • Usage data (pages visited, features used)

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve our services
  • To personalize trip recommendations using AI
  • To send notifications you've opted into (email, SMS)
  • To facilitate group coordination among trip members

4. SMS/Text Messaging

If you provide your phone number and enable SMS notifications, we will send you text messages related to trip activity (e.g., new chat messages from your group). Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency varies based on trip activity. You can opt out at any time by:

  • Replying STOP to any message
  • Disabling SMS notifications in your profile settings

We will not share your phone number with third parties for marketing purposes. For help, reply HELP to any message or contact us.

5. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only:

  • With other members of trips you join (name, airport, votes, availability, chat messages)
  • With service providers that help us operate (Google Cloud, Twilio, Anthropic)
  • When required by law

6. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures including encrypted connections (HTTPS), secure credential storage (Google Cloud Secret Manager), and access controls. No method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, but we do our best to protect your data.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and associated data via our contact form.

8. Cookies

We use session cookies to keep you signed in. We do not use third-party advertising cookies.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We'll notify you of significant changes via email or in-app notice.

10. Contact

Questions? Contact us.
