## 8. The lessons that compound across sessions

Things to remember about this domain so we don't re-discover them:

1. **II's auth is the only Akamai wall.** Once authed, every member-area
   endpoint is plain HTTP. Headless login is dead end (playbook §12);
   cookie replay from real Chrome is the path.
2. **Different deposit-sides unlock different inventory pools.** K5133
   full 2BR pulls one set; K5133S 1BR master pulls a slightly different
   (and sometimes better) set; K5133R studio is its own pool. Always
   sweep all 3 sides for any decision.
3. **Wide-window searches return only the soonest available week per
   resort.** To enumerate ALL weeks at a resort, you need to step
   through narrower windows.
4. **Rebrand "deposited" vs "available to deposit"** — the former is
   committed in II's pool, the latter is still under owner control.
   Read the My Units status text carefully before assuming.
5. **Royal Sands resale value is ~purchase price after 22 years.** That's
   unusual — most timeshares lose 80-95%. Royal Resorts' captive renter
   demand and 4.9/5 reviews keep the market liquid.
6. **Redweek + Koala are the canonical owner-direct rental channels.**
   Redweek has more liquidity (84 active 2BR Royal Sands rentals);
   Koala is free to list. Cross-list both.
7. **The Reddit signal that A-tier inventory routes to Getaways is
   load-bearing for any future scraper.** If we ever fix the Getaway
   parser, that's where premium Hawaii hides at $329 within 30 days.

**Why Hawaii inventory is structurally empty in II's exchange pool:**

1. **Hawaii owners don't deposit** (TUG forum validated): *"anyone who

   - Internal Marriott-to-Marriott exchanges (only Marriott home-resort

   - Direct booking channels (Marriott.com, Expedia, Booking)
3. **Royal Resorts is non-Marriott family.** External traders land in the

4. **Trade power doesn't matter** when the pool is structurally empty. K5133 is
   high-trade-power for what it is, but it can't pull weeks owners never
   deposited in the first place.

**Therefore §1 headline stands:** Royal Sands K5133 cannot exchange into
a cool beachfront sleeps-6 Hawaii resort June-Nov 2026. KNK Molokai is
